Bioplastics Definition

Bioplastics are renewable polymers such as PLA derived from biomass.

Petroleum Plastics Definition

Petroleum plastics are synthetic polymers derived from crude oil.

Chemical Structure of Bioplastics

Bioplastics contain ester functional groups formed via condensation polymerization.

Chemical Structure of Petroleum Plastics

Long hydrocarbon backbone with strong C–C covalent bonds.

Impact in Today’s World

Plastic pollution contributes to microplastics and environmental damage.

Conclusion

Bioplastics offer sustainability benefits but face cost challenges.

Message to the World

We must move toward sustainable materials and reduce single-use plastics.
